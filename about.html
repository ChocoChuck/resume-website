Interests

Music

I've always had a great interest in music. Growing up my father played 101.1 on the radio all the time and if you don't know what the station does, it plays New Yorks greatest hits in the 70s, 80s and 90s. So I grew up with a very old sense of music and its stuck with me to this day. Of course I still listen to my generation's music but I'm if you saw my spotify playlist you'd think I'm a very old man. "Earth Wind And Fire", "The Temptaions", "Queen", the music back then really said a lot. These days, not so much. My love for music drove me to make some of my own. While it's just a hobby, I take it very seriously because music is the most powerful art form.
